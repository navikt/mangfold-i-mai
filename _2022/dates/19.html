  • Navigating Posten.no without sight

    9:30 til 10:15 [Arranged by Posten] Get a demonstration of screen reader usage on the Posten.no website. A screen reader is a tool which reads and interprets the content on the screen so a visually impaired person can access the information. Presented by Christian Thon of the Norwegian Association for the Blind.
