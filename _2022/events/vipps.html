How Vipps works to ensure inclusive design in everyday work

  • Torsdag 19. mai 2022
  • 10:15 - 11:00
  • Teams
  • Åpent for hele verden

What’s this presentation about?

The talk is about how Vipps vent from a service that excluded users with sight impairments to winning awards for our universal design work. Relevant for everyone.

Who’s this presentation for?

Suitable for everyone who is curious about Vipps’s journey from excluding to winning universal design.

Foredrags­holderne

Arranged by Posten
Presented by Max Schrøder, Head of Design at Vipps

Mangfold i mai er en arrangementsserie rundt mangfold, inkludering og universell utforming i regi av Team Universell utforming og inkluderende design hos Arbeids- og velferdsdirektoratet.
